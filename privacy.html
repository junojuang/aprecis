Privacy Policy

Aprecis · Effective 22 May 2026

Aprecis is a microlearning app that turns AI research papers into short, swipeable lessons. This policy explains what we collect, why, and the choices you have. We keep data collection to the minimum the app needs to work.

What we collect

When you sign in with Sign in with Apple, we receive your name and an email address from Apple. If you choose Apple's private email relay, we only ever see the relay address, not your real email. We use this to create and maintain your account and to sync your reading progress and saved papers across your devices.

We do not collect your contacts, location, photos, browsing history, or advertising identifiers. We do not use third-party analytics or advertising SDKs.

How we use it

  • To create and secure your account.
  • To save your shelf, reading goal, and progress so they follow you across devices.
  • To respond to support requests you send us.

We do not use your data for advertising, and we do not track you across other apps or websites.

Where it is stored

Account data is stored on our backend infrastructure (Supabase), protected in transit and at rest. We retain it for as long as your account is active.

Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share it only with the service providers that host our infrastructure, and only so they can run the service on our behalf. We may disclose data if required by law.

Your choices

You can request a copy of your data or ask us to delete your account and all associated data at any time. Email us and we will action the request within 30 days. Deleting your account removes your name, email, and reading history from our systems.

Children

Aprecis is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from them.

Changes

If this policy changes, we will update the effective date above and, for material changes, notify you in the app.
