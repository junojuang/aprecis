Card 02 · Core idea
Three things that break at once
0/3 opened
i
Attention concentrates on one token
Softmax across the head's 512 tokens collapses — one position gets weight ≈ 1, the rest ≈ 0. Normally spread is ~0.2 per cluster.
⌄
ii
Downstream layers lose signal
With 95%+ of attention on one token, the residual stream for other positions gets starved. Layer N+1 builds features from near-zero vectors.
⌄
iii
Model doubles down in confidence
The logit for the predicted token is sharpened, not widened — so the model outputs the wrong answer with higher confidence than a balanced head would.

Reading the flow as a whole

Aprecis isn't a reader, a feed, or a course. It's a 2-minute daily appointment with one idea, wrapped in an interactive surface that makes reading feel like tapping. Every screen on this board defends that promise.

The three jobs

  • Capture attention — the hook card and streak flame compete with TikTok for the same 2 minutes.
  • Transfer a single idea — each deck is designed around one load-bearing sentence.
  • Build a habit — streak, level, glossary are receipts that compound over weeks.

What's deliberately missing

  • No infinite feed. "Explore" is a drawer, not the home screen.
  • No comments or social. Removes the outrage vector entirely.
  • No PDF reader. arXiv is the reward link — app stays light.
  • No push beyond 1/day. Respect is the moat.

What to build next

  • Voice deck — the same 6 cards as a 90-second audio summary for commutes.
  • Weekly digest email — the 5 takeaway sentences from the week in serif italic.
  • Share card — export the takeaway sentence as a 9:16 image for social.
  • Paper clusters — when you finish 3 related decks, unlock a "concept map" review.
